Visual Emotional Impact

How our visual choices create immediate emotional responses that support mental wellness and reduce anxiety for diverse users.

Calming Blue Palette

Cool blue tones immediately signal trust and tranquility, crucial for users experiencing anxiety or seeking mental health support. Research shows blue reduces cortisol levels and promotes feelings of safety.

High Contrast Accessibility

Strong color contrast ensures Akasha and other users with visual challenges can immediately distinguish interactive elements, reducing frustration and cognitive load.

Cultural Neutrality

Blue transcends cultural boundaries without triggering negative associations, supporting Denisho's multicultural background and creating inclusive first impressions.

Typography Psychology

How font choices create immediate comfort and reduce barriers to engagement for users with varying technological confidence.

Modern Sans-Serif

Red Hat Text provides professional clarity while remaining approachable for users like Akasha who need clear interfaces. Its modern design appeals to younger users while maintaining excellent readability for all age groups.

Generous Sizing

Large headings and comfortable body text immediately signal accessibility, making the platform feel welcoming rather than intimidating for users seeking help. Red Hat Text's open letterforms enhance readability at all sizes.

Clear Hierarchy

Distinct size differences create instant understanding of content organization, reducing anxiety about navigating complex mental health information. Red Hat Text's variable font weights provide excellent hierarchical distinction.
