Design Principles

  • Accessibility First: Every design decision considers users with varying technological comfort and visual abilities
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Colors and patterns respect diverse cultural backgrounds and avoid triggering associations
  • Emotional Safety: Visual hierarchy and color choices promote psychological safety for vulnerable users
  • Progressive Enhancement: Core functionality works across all devices and connection speeds
