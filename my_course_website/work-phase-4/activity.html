Mindfulness & Meditation Workshop

A gentle introduction to mindfulness practices for mental wellbeing and stress reduction

About This Workshop

Join us for a peaceful evening of mindfulness and meditation practice. This beginner-friendly workshop is designed to introduce you to simple yet powerful techniques that can help reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance overall wellbeing.

Whether you're completely new to meditation or looking to deepen your practice, you'll find a welcoming and supportive environment. Our experienced facilitator will guide you through gentle breathing exercises, body awareness techniques, and guided meditation sessions.

No special equipment or prior experience is needed. Just bring yourself and an open mind. This workshop is part of our ongoing commitment to making mental health support accessible to everyone in our community.
