About Our Platform

We are building a supportive community where everyone can access mental health resources, friendly connections, and find path to wellness.

Who We Are

We're a diverse team of mental health advocates, community organizers, and tech professionals. Some of us have lived through mental health challenges ourselves, others have supported loved ones through theirs.

Together, we're working to break down stigma and make support more accessible for everyone.

Diverse Team Advocates Lived Experience Tech Experts

Our Dream

Imagine mental health support as easy as texting a friend. No complex systems to navigate, no judgment, just genuine connection when you need it most.

Accessible No Stigma

Our Commitment

People before profit, always. We prioritize accessibility, protect your privacy, support peer-led initiatives, and share evidence-based resources. Your feedback shapes how we grow.

Our Values

Honesty, respect, and trust guide everything we do. We're transparent about how we operate, embrace diverse perspectives, and back up our words with consistent action.

"Mental health support should never be a commodity."

No Advertising: You will never see advertisements on our platform. We don't sell ad space, run sponsored content, or promote commercial products.

No Data Sales: We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Your privacy is not negotiable.

How We're Funded: Our platform is funded through grants, community donations, and support from Connect Communities Initiative. Every dollar goes toward maintaining our services, not generating profit.

"We believe that strong communities are built on genuine connections and mutual support."

Connect Communities Initiative (CCI) is a non-profit organization founded in 2020 with a mission to strengthen social cohesion across Australia. We work with diverse communities—from cultural groups to wellness advocates—to create platforms that amplify voices, share resources, and foster meaningful connections.

This wellness community platform is one of several initiatives under CCI's umbrella. Our other projects include cultural heritage preservation platforms, environmental advocacy networks, and regional community hubs. What unites all our work is a commitment to accessibility, inclusivity, and community governance.
